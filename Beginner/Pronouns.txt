Eclipsinian Pronouns
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Person:

he: mulle (mool-le)
she: soramulle (soar-ah-mool-le)
you: yaugva/yagva (yah-ug-vah/yaug-vah)
they: tormulleya/tulya (tohr-mool-lay-yah)
we: talle (tahl-ay)
me: mule/me (muh-le/meh)

Having:

his: multesor/mutes (mool-teh-soar/moo-tehs)
her: mullebrel (mool-le-brehl)
hers: mullebrelsor/mullebres (mool-le-brehl-soar/mool-le-brehs)
